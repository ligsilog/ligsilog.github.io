About Me

Welcome to my portfolio!

I'm Joyce, a freelance content writer, graphic artist, and designer. I began my freelancing journey as an SEO content writer in 2018, using writing as a way to earn extra income while still in college. My Biology degree has been a valuable asset in my writings, allowing me to confidently write about medicine, technology, and pharmaceuticals—areas that have become my niche.

After graduating, I launched my passion project, VIXEN Crafts, which gave me the opportunity to explore social media marketing and refine my copywriting skills. I experienced firsthand how well-crafted copies can influence customer decisions and drive product and service sales. In the process, I also delved into graphic design to enhance the visibility and impact of these posts.

Today, I use the skills I’ve developed to help small businesses expand their reach while continuing to build my own professional brand.
