Silymarin in milk thistle extract
Milk thistle or Silybum marianum is a plant known for its active ingredient called silymarin. Silymarin is a substance that is widely studied for its estrogenic effects and with the help of milk thistle, estrogen detox is made possible.

Uses and Benefits of Milk Thistle Extract

MTE as a natural antioxidant

The antioxidants’ role in our body is to eliminate the harmful free radicals in our body. We get these free radicals through exposure from the following:

• Environmental pollution
• Alcohol intake
• Smoking
• X-rays
• Ultraviolet rays from the sun
• Industrial chemicals

Abundance of free radicals, in our body can cause oxidative stress which is a condition that is highly connected to the development of certain types of cancer, inflammatory diseases, ulcers, and neurological disorders.

Our body can fight these radicals through antioxidants acquired from our diet and other supplements. As an antioxidant, the silymarin in milk thistle extract not only eliminates but also prevents the formation of harmful free radicals in our body.

MTE as treatment for liver diseases
Nowadays, milk thistle extract is mainly used to assist the treatment of liver diseases. Studies have shown that consumption of silymarin has metabolic effects that aid in the reconstruction of liver tissues. It also eliminates toxins in the liver brought by excessive alcohol consumption. Other than liver diseases caused by alcohol, effects of silymarin in the treatment of hepatitis and liver cirrhosis are also being studied.

MTE for estrogen detox
Unusually high levels of excess estrogen can be unsafe. In men, it can cause prostate cancer and BPH or benign prostatic hyperplasia. Other minor effects of estrogen dominance in men are:

• gynecomastia or having “man boobs”
• Low libido
• Erectile dysfunction
• Anxiety and depression
• Weight gain
• Diabetes (type 2)

If you are already experiencing estrogen dominance, it is best to consume soybeans and soy products with caution along with other food that are known to increase estrogen levels.

Although milk thistle extracts (mte) are known for its estrogenic effects, its phytoestrogen is not strong enough to increase the risk for breast cancer. Its anti-estrogenic effects are actually much greater. The silymarin found in the extract binds to estrogen receptors to block the effects of strong estrogenic agents such as estradiol. By blocking strong estrogenic agents, it can lessen the production of estrogen in the body.

Our liver is basically the one responsible for maintaining normal levels of hormones in our body. However, this can be altered due to increase in fat percentage and aging. Since silymarin mainly affects the liver, it directly affects the liver to detoxify the body from excess estrogen. That is why, aside from treating liver disease, milk thistle extract is notable for estrogen detox.

Side effects of using Milk Thistle Extract
Using milk thistle extract is generally safe to use and gastrointestinal side effects are rarely recorded. However, it can contraindicate some medicines taken for diabetes and may reduce the effect of antibiotics.
It is still best and recommended to consult your doctor if you want to start taking the extract.
