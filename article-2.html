Switching to organic
cbd oil as a daily supplement

The benefits of hemp plant extracts are gaining popularity in the recent years. And so far, organic cbd oil is the most promising.

The use of hemp products has been shrouded in social stigma because of the marijuana plant’s legal status. Though lately, it has been proven that not all substances found in marijuana can make you high.

CBD is a cannabinoid found in Cannabis sativa that doesn’t have psychoactive effects. It is a compound that indirectly affects the cannabinoid receptors in our body unlike the other compound which is thc or tetrahydrocannabinol.

THC, as compared to cbd (cannabidiol), overwhelmingly binds with the cannabinoid receptors in our body. As a result, it throws off the balance of neurotransmitters in our brain which gives the feeling of being “high”.

When this happens, neural processes that involves memory and alertness are impaired. Prolonged exposure to the chemical may cause addiction, memory impairment, and in some cases, mental health issues.

Compared to THC, CBD doesn’t bind with the cannabinoid receptors. Rather, it lets our body’s naturally occurring cannabinoids do their job.

This indirect stimulation of the cannabinoid receptors amplifies the effects of the natural cannabinoids in our body. In addition, cbd also affects other receptors in our body in various ways:

• CBD can directly affect serotonin receptors which leads to feelings of calmness and reduce anxiety and stress levels.

• CBD is also known to interact with vallinoid receptors which are responsible for handling inflammation in our bodies, regulation of body temperature, and lessen pain.

• CBD acts as an antagonist to the “orphan receptor” GPR55 that is linked to the proliferation of cancer cells.

• CBD can help in the treatment of Alzheimer’s disease since it also activates the PPAR-gamma receptors or the receptors responsible for the degradation of the substances linked to neurological disorders.

It is important to note that THC is not all bad since it also gives the same benefits that cbd can. However, it should be accompanied by CBD to counteract its overwhelming binding properties with the cannabinoid receptors in our body.

Keeping it organic!
It should be noted that organic products should come from naturally grown plants that are not cultivated with the use of pesticides and harmful fertilizers. Organically grown plants are considered to be healthier than their industrial counterparts.

Pesticides and fertilizers can pollute plants with heavy metals. These metals sometimes remain even after the extraction process and thus contaminating the end product that is given to the consumers.

Presence of heavy metals in the product and exposure to it can be detrimental to your health. That is why you should choose cbd oil products that are guaranteed to have come from organically grown hemp plant.

Also, the process of extraction must keep the integrity of the organic product.

The overall production of the oil must not use commercialized harmful solvents.

Though some brands remove these solvents from the formula, it is not guaranteed that there will be no residuals in the final product. So, when deciding to buy from a brand, make sure that they use organic solvents in the extraction process as well.
